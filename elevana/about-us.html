About Elevana

Welcome to Elevana –
Helping You Make Dreams Come True

Embarking on the journey to a new home is a story of dreams and aspirations.

At Elevana, we understand the emotional investment that goes into buying a new home – the weeks and months of contemplation, the excitement of choosing the perfect development, and the joy of making it your own. However, the true magic happens when a homeowner first walks through their new property, and that moment of truth must align with the dream you've sold them.

Unfortunately, the path to this dream is not always smooth. The complexities of the homebuying process, from multiple meetings with developers, mortgage companies, and solicitors, to the anticipation built by glamorous show homes, can lead to elevated expectations. As the day of handover approaches, the fear of shattered dreams looms large if the reality falls short.

This is where Elevana steps in – your trusted partner in turning dreams into reality.

Our mission is to ensure that the excitement and anticipation cultivated throughout the homebuying journey culminate in a seamless and delightful experience at the critical point of delivery. Elevana is not just a service provider; we are the architects of exceptional new home demonstrations and handover experiences, designed to leave a lasting impression on your homebuyers.

Why Choose Elevana?

With over 25 years of experience in the construction sector, Elevana brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project. We're a group of individuals with diverse experiences, united by a single mission: to delight our clients and their customers.

From marketing and market research, training course development and delivery, policy and procedure development, conference speaking, article writing, audio recording, video creation, radio presenting, and more. Our collective background shapes a unique set of services, covering everything from pre-demonstration home inspections to post-handover customer support.

Over the past 10 years, we've delivered over 8,000 new home demonstrations and handovers, creating hundreds of Instructional Videos and Easy User Guides. On top of that, members of the Elevana team have trained over 36,000 people in how to demonstrate and handover a new home since the first course in 2002. We use this unmatched level of expertise and experience to deliver the very best service and the highest levels of consistency.

Our Promise To You:

At Elevana we commit to delivering consistent, high-quality services, ensuring that every new homeowner’s dream is realised.

We are proud of the relationships developed with clients, the majority of whom we’ve worked with for many years. From multinational construction companies to smaller regional housebuilders, regardless of size, we guarantee that you and your customers will get exceptional service at every stage of the homebuyer journey.

Meet The Team

Malcolm Pitcher

Managing Director

After many years in the motor industry, culminating in my role as European Marketing Manager for Honda cars, I took my first steps into house building as Group Marketing Director for Wimpey Homes, where my focus was squarely on “putting the customer at the centre of the business”.

In 1998, after five years with Wimpey I started my own consultancy business, focusing on the housing sector. Since then, I have conducted thousands of customer satisfaction surveys, written articles for major publications, been a key-note conference speaker, consultant and trainer specialising in customer service.

As Managing Director of Elevana I have the great privilege to lead an exceptional team that continues to inspire me every day, teaching me far more than I could ever teach them.

Outside of work my passions include walking, keeping fit, studying human behaviours, cooking and wishing I’d been a gigging rock musician for just a little bit longer!

Malcolm walking near Sommaroya, Norway

Cary Davey

Divisional Director

I’ve spent most of my career in marketing including managing the #1Club Gold Hertz rental car programme across Europe, which provided me with valuable experience in dealing with multi-cultural societies and different working environments. Going back into the construction industry offered me the opportunity of supporting the launch of Osmagold plastic piping across the building, developer and merchant networks.

Being made redundant in 2000 allowed me to freelance but also support Malcolm with various projects. After a couple of years, my training skills as a gliding instructor came in very handy in setting up the home demonstration and handover training business which has gone from strength to strength, year on year. This led me to set up a team of Elite Demonstrators in 2014 who undertake home inspections, home demonstrations and handovers supported by instruction videos. Initially in London and the southeast, the team has expanded nationwide.

Having retired from gliding I can now focus on my love of photography. And when I’m not out walking or photographing something, I’m doing agility competitions with my border collie.

Cary test flying a classic Tiger Moth at Bicester

Shaun Buswell

Head of Elevana AV

One of the things I most like about Elevana is its ability to adapt to changes.

I joined during the early stages of Elite Demonstrators and witnessed its evolution from training, to fulfilling the rising demand for physical demonstrations and handovers, to creating audio-visual solutions, to where we are now - providing an entire aftersales solution.

Alex Pitcher

Business Operations Manager

Originally from Swindon, I spent 11 years at In-house Research, starting with data analysis before quickly moving into managing the company’s systems and databases.

In my spare time, I discovered a love for amateur dramatics, performing in dozens of shows around Wiltshire. That passion led me to London for professional training, and I took an 8-year "career break" from 2016 to 2024 to pursue the performing arts.

To help fund my studies, I worked part-time as a demonstrator for Elevana. After drama school, I launched a successful voiceover business, landing projects with some major companies like Google.

Post-Covid, in 2021, I returned to Elevana conducting virtual home demonstrations.

In 2023, my first daughter was born, and after those tricky first 6 months, it felt like the right time to return to full-time work. In March 2024, Elevana entrusted me with leading a large project in Central London. My current role is Business Operations Manager.

Now based in West London, in my spare time - when I’m not chasing after my toddler - I still do the occasional voiceover from my home studio.

Sam O'Neill

Training Administrator

Joining Elevana in 2012 after seven years in the Army, I started as an administrator and now serve as Training Administrator.

During my time in the Army, which included postings to Germany and tours of Iraq and Afghanistan, I organised the paperwork and the briefing of soldiers flying into and out of the country. Flights could be delayed or cancelled at short notice for operational reasons and I had to learn how to deal with the stress and distress felt by soldiers of all ranks when such situations occurred. Especially when they were waiting to fly into difficult situations or get back home to see their families.

This experience honed my administration skills and gave me a lot of confidence to be able to work with people of all levels and be able to deal with changes quickly and seamlessly with no issue.

Outside of work I love spending time with my family, going for walks or baking with my girls. A particular hobby of mine is reading, whenever you see me, I will always have a book on the go.

Project Manager
