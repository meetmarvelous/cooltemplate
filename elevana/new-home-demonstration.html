New Home Demonstration & Handover Training

Master the Art of Home Demonstrations and Handovers with Elevana's Expert Training

A poor new home demonstration or handover can not only shatter the customer's dreams but also tarnish your reputation. Elevana's Home Demonstration and Handover Training ensures that your team is equipped with the skills and confidence to deliver exceptional customer experiences at the two most critical stages of the customer journey.

Our meticulously designed hands-on course covers the fundamentals of conducting successful home demonstrations and handovers. From emphasising the importance of preparation to delivering detailed yet captivating experiences for your customers, we leave no stone unturned.

The most important part of our training is the in person home demonstration walkthrough, conducted in one of your properties and tailored to your specifications, policies, and procedures.

Read Testimonials

During Our Training Sessions, Your Team Will Learn:

  • How to inspect the property through the eyes of the customer to make sure that it is ready for the big occasion.
  • Techniques to prepare customers for the experience to come, ensuring that they know when and where to arrive, who should attend and how long it will take.
  • Personalisation strategies for different customer profiles, be they first-time buyers, seasoned investors, or tenants.
  • Effective demonstration methods for every finish and feature of a customer’s new home, giving them the confidence to operate, maintain, and, most of all … enjoy it.
  • Strategies for relaying comprehensive information to customers, the depth of information you need to relay and where to find it.
  • Presentation skills to captivate customers and maintain focus throughout the walkthrough.
  • How to manage the property walkthrough so that everything is presented in a logical sequence.
  • Handling of customer emotions and perceptions during demonstrations and handovers.
  • Strategies to make every customer feel valued and special, not just another statistic.

Our Unique Approach

Our unique approach combining online learning with hands on, interactive sessions in your properties ensures an immersive and engaging experience for all involved.

We also offer refresher training to update your team's skills and ensure they can demonstrate any new property features effectively.

With Elevana's New Home Demonstration and Handover Training, your team will be well-equipped to tackle any challenges, ensuring professional and seamless experiences for your customers.
