New Home Instructional, Explainer and Interactive Videos

With over 35 years of construction industry experience, Elevana is the go-to choice for instructional, explainer, and interactive video production

With over 35 years of construction industry experience, Elevana is the go-to choice for instructional, explainer, and interactive video production.

The power of video is huge - preferred by 80% of people over written text – it’s a great way to convey information to your customers at every stage of their buyer journey.

Whether it's fully interactive video walkthroughs with a virtual host, allowing users to explore properties room by room, or New Home Instructional and Explainer Videos providing 24/7 support, Elevana understands your customers' needs, making us the ideal partner for creating compelling video content for your homebuyers.

Our videos are also a great resource for your Customer Service teams, helping them to resolve customer issues quickly and easily.

Benefits Of Using Our Videos

  • Enhanced customer satisfaction: Instant access to information, no waiting in the call queue or having to phone at certain times to get answers.
  • Multi-lingual videos reduce customer and support team frustration
  • Reduced customer care calls: Enable your team to focus on resolving complex issues by addressing common queries through our videos.
  • Minimized risk of property damage: Proper usage guidance reduces the likelihood of costly mistakes.
  • Knowledge base support: Your team will have access to this valuable resource, which will help them effectively deal with customer queries.

Why Choose Elevana?

In-depth expertise

With over 8,000 demonstrations and handovers behind us, we know the questions and issues that customers are likely to come to you with. This means that we don't need script writers or directors, saving you both time and cost.

Quicker set-ups, quicker results

Elevana’s experienced team employ techniques that do not require large amounts of equipment or people on site to achieve professional results.

Multi-lingual capability

We can deliver videos in multiple languages. Eliminating the potential for misunderstanding and misuse of the home.

Hosting capability

We can host your videos, ensuring that they are always available and up to date.

Cost-effective solution

We are confident in front of a camera when explaining any part of a new home. You don't need to hire expensive actors, pay release fees or train your own staff to do this.

Uninterrupted workflow

Our in-studio voiceover recording capabilities mean that construction activities can continue seamlessly whilst we film and produce high-quality content.
